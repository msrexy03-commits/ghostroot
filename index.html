The manifesto

AI has two problems nobody is solving. The first is energy — data centers burning fossil fuels at scale while the industry calls itself the future. The second is privacy — your conversations, your thoughts, your data being logged, analyzed, and sold back to you as advertising.

People accept this because they have no alternative.

GhostRoot is the alternative. Not more powerful. Not louder. Just honest — about what it uses, what it keeps, and what it forgets. Which is everything.

Data policy
Zero logging
Ephemeral processing only. Conversations processed in memory, response generated, everything discarded. Nothing written to persistent storage. Ever.
Zero monetization
Your data is never sold, shared, or used for advertising. We make money from subscriptions. The business model is aligned with your privacy, not against it.
Zero training
Your conversations never train the model unless you explicitly opt in. We ask. We never assume. Consent is a feature, not a checkbox buried in terms of service.
Verifiable
Open source infrastructure. You can verify the policy is real, not just stated. We publish energy usage, model architecture, and data handling. Trust should be earned.
